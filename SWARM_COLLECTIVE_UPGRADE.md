# Swarm Collective Upgrade: Hierarchical Excellence (v3.5)

We have successfully upgraded the ZilMate Swarm from a flat specialist model to a **Hierarchical Digital Corporation**.

## Key Enhancements

### 1. The 7 Departmental Heads
We have introduced a leadership layer that manages specialist agents, reducing the CEO's cognitive load:
- **Strategy Head (CSO):** Owns the roadmap and mission alignment.
- **CTO:** Owns technical delivery, code quality, and infrastructure.
- **CMO:** Owns growth, brand, and customer acquisition.
- **CRO:** Owns revenue, sales pipelines, and MRR.
- **Operations Head (COO):** Owns business processes, logistics, and CSAT.
- **CISO:** Owns security posture, compliance, and auditing.
- **CDO:** Owns data governance, insights, and quantitative truth.

### 2. Hierarchical Memory (Corporate Brain)
Agents are no longer "amnesiac specialists." They now have access to:
- **Corporate Notebook:** A global source of truth for vision and strategic goals.
- **Departmental Memory:** Isolated workspaces where departments log their internal progress and SOPs.

### 3. Autonomous Delegation (Tool-Based Collaboration)
Agents can now collaborate without manual routing:
- `delegateTask`: Specialists can "ping" other specialists for help.
- `readCorporateContext`: All agents can align their work with the higher-level vision.

## How it Works
1. The **CEO** sets a goal (e.g., "Increase revenue by 20%").
2. The **CRO** reads the goal, analyzes revenue leaks, and delegates lead sourcing to **Sales Ops**.
3. **Sales Ops** uses Composio to find leads and reports back to the **CRO's** departmental memory.
4. The **CRO** synthesizes this into a report for the **CEO**.

This structure ensures ZilMate can scale to handle complex, multi-week business projects with minimal human intervention.
