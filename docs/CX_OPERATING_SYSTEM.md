# 📞 Customer Experience (CX) & Retention Operating System

This document defines the proactive strategy for managing customer relationships and maximizing retention within the ZilMate Digital Corporation.

## 1. The "Churn Sentinel" Protocol
Instead of waiting for tickets, the **CX & Retention Specialist** proactively monitors for signs of customer dissatisfaction.

### Monitoring Points:
- **Usage Drop-off:** Correlating Mixpanel data for declining activity.
- **Payment Friction:** Identifying recurring Stripe payment failures or dunning events.
- **Negative Sentiment Spikes:** Analyzing Social Media and Support Channel sentiment via Web Intelligence.

## 2. Feedback-to-Product Loop
The CX specialist acts as the primary conduit between the user and the **Product Manager**.

### Process:
1. **Insight Harvesting:** Weekly synthesis of support tickets using the "Jobs to be Done" (JTBD) framework.
2. **Feature Request Scoring:** Categorizing requests by frequency, impact on MRR, and strategic alignment.
3. **The "Voice of Customer" Report:** A monthly brief for the **Strategy Head (CSO)** and **CEO**.

## 3. High-Touch Engagement
For Enterprise or "Power User" segments, the CX specialist executes high-touch workflows:
- **Case Study Sourcing:** Identifying successful users and triggering the **Content Writer** for testimonials.
- **Proactive Outreach:** Reaching out to users with failed payments or low usage before they churn.

## 4. Crisis & Support Resolution
- **First Contact Resolution (FCR):** A target of 85%+ resolution on the first interaction.
- **Sentiment-Based Escalation:** Tickets with "Angry" or "Urgent" sentiment are automatically escalated to the **Head of Operations**.
- **Self-Service Expansion:** CX constantly updates the **Corporate Wiki** and customer-facing FAQs to reduce ticket volume.
